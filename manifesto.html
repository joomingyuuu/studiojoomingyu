Manifesto

We make things to notice beauty.

We believe the world is already beautiful. Beauty is born not from perfect uniformity, but from a healthy form of diversity.

We create books, films, writings, comics, websites, objects, and experiments. Though the medium changes, the question remains: How can people better discover the beauty of the world?

Technology is a material, not a destination.

Technology is not a destination, but a material. What matters is not the mere use of new tools, but what human senses we can bring into sharper focus through them.
