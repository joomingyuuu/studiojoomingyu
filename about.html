About

Hello. I'm Mingyu.

I build projects that help people notice the beauty already present in the world. Some become books. Some become films. Some become websites. Some remain unfinished forever.

Studio Joomingyu is not a fully-fledged company yet; it is an open workspace evolving step-by-step for upcoming books, films, and projects.

Studio Joomingyu is an experimental one-person studio fusing culture, art, and experimentation—treating technology not as a destination, but as a material to bring human senses into sharper focus. We carry out a diverse range of projects, including books, films, and comics. My studio is open to everyone. Any activity that is engaging and meaningful is always welcome.

Projects – I undertake various projects that I find compelling and meaningful. I am currently crafting tools to discover the beauty of the world in multifaceted ways.

Notes – An online magazine publishing writings on a wide spectrum of topics. It spans diverse subjects such as interviews, art, philosophy, and science.

Archive – A space displaying everything that has breathed inspiration into my studio: scenes from films that struck a chord while working, passages excerpted from books, project sketches, rough comic storyboards, website coding errors, traces of film script revisions, and single lines of fleeting ideas. I hope you, too, find diverse forms of inspiration as you wander through this Archive.
